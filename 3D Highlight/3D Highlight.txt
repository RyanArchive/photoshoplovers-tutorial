3D Highlight

1. Duplicate the background (Ctrl + J)
2. Filter > Distort > Zigzag
3. Amount = 50; Ridges = 10
4. Filter > Stylize > Extrude
5. Size = 20; Depth = 200
6. Duplicate layer (Ctrl + J)
7. Filter > Stylize > Find Edges
8. Invert (Ctrl + I)
9. Change the Blending Mode to Screen
10. Select layer below
11. Create new fill or adjustment layer > Curves
